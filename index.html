SlippiTV

A new way to watch.

Watch your friends play Slippi netplay in real time

Download SlippiTV

No additional lag

Streams only the .slp data. If your potato can play netplay, it can run SlippiTV.

Runs in the background

Run it once and forget about it. SlippiTV streams your games from the background so you don't have to remember.

Realtime spectating

Watch games as they happen, with minimal latency. SlippiTV can even notify you when your friends are live.
